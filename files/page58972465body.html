Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data that may be collected while visiting our website. This Privacy Policy applies to all users, regardless of their country of residence.
We do not collect, store or process your personal data (such as your name, email address or phone number). However, we use third-party analytics and advertising tools that automatically collect certain information to analyze visits to our site and improve its performance.

Services we use:
• Google Analytics
• Google Tag Manager
• Yandex Metrica

These services may collect the following data:
• Partially anonymized IP address.
• Device type (mobile, tablet, PC).
• Browser, operating system and screen resolution.
• Geolocation (country, city, determined based on IP).
• Traffic source (search engines, advertising campaigns, direct access).
• Behavior on the site (pages viewed, time on the site, clicks and scrolls).
The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:
  • Analyzing site traffic and understanding audience preferences.
  • Optimizing content and user experience.
  • Improving the performance of advertising campaigns.

We do not transfer data to third parties, except for the above-mentioned services used for analytics and advertising.
We comply with international privacy standards, including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California, USA) and other laws.
